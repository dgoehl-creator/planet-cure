The PlanetCure Story

The $10 Treatment Your Dermatologist
Probably Never Mentioned

A Phase 3 randomized controlled trial was published in the New England Journal of Medicine. It found that a simple, over-the-counter vitamin B3 supplement — taken twice daily — reduced new skin cancer diagnoses by 23% compared to placebo. A 2025 study in JAMA Dermatology found up to 54% reduced risk in high-risk patients.

Nicotinamide. $10–15/month at any pharmacy. No prescription needed. Backed by an NEJM Phase 3 trial. Your dermatologist almost certainly never mentioned it. Not because it doesn't work. Because no one profits from telling you to take a cheap vitamin.

This story — a cheap, accessible, evidence-backed option that doesn't get promoted because it's not profitable to promote — repeats across every condition we've researched. That's why PlanetCure exists.

We find the things that work but don't get promoted. We put them next to the expensive options and show you the evidence for each. You decide.
